About Me

This is what I do.

I am a C.S Undergrad at BITS Goa. This beautiful picture shown above is my college campus. I enjoy coding and solving challenging questions. I am also interested in Web Development and Graphic Designing. I find Astronomy quite interesting and enjoy astronomical observations. I like arts and crafts as I’m a creative person. I enjoy being physically active as well, and spend a lot of time playing basketball.

I am a core member of SEDS Celestia, the Astronomy Club of BITS Goa and started writing articles for it's blog the Wandering Star recently. My first article was a record-breaker for the most number of views. I was amazed by it's unexpected success. I realized that if a complex topic is explained in simple words, it can become quite interesting.

One of my favorite quotes is by Leonardo da Vinci: “Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication”. The way I understand it is that simplicity goes hand in hand with perfection. It shows how beautiful and elegant things are when they are not lost in the detail. It means sifting through tons of soil to find one perfect gem.

So, let's keep it Simple!
